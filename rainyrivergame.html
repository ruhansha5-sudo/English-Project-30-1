Tim stares at the paper. He believes the war is wrong. But the whole town, his family, his country, is watching him.

The Meatpacking Plant

A mirror of the war waiting for him

Working in the factory, Tim feels it. The noise. Something in him compelled him to make a decision; he will go north. To Canada.

Driving North

Every mile north is a mile from his old self.

Tim gets in his car and drives.
